\# 4. WEEK STRUCTURE



\## RULES

\- 3 ON days (training)

\- 4 OFF days (rest/cardio)

\- meals chosen only from Meal Blocks

\- no ad-hoc food decisions outside system



\---



\## ON DAY STRUCTURE



meal\_1: breakfast (oats + whey + banana optional)

meal\_2: work snack

meal\_3: lunch (A/B/C)

meal\_4: skyr + marmellata + corn flakes

meal\_5: dinner (A/B/C)



\---



\## OFF DAY STRUCTURE



meal\_1: breakfast (oats + whey + chocolate)

meal\_2: work snack

meal\_3: lunch (A/B/C)

meal\_4: skyr + marmellata

meal\_5: dinner (A/B/C/D)
